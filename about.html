What is


The Audio Blanket Experience?


Original artwork by Rich Rosato


Audio blanket is a live audio visual show/experience using cutting edge proffesional dj equipment and state of the art lighting for an immersive experience. Playing a blend of chill edm, orchestral, ambient and original music the audio is mixed in a way that engages the senses.The music is accompanied by spoken word and philosophy from some of our worlds greatest minds both past and present.
A finely tuned video is projected and played alongside the audio in high definition with dramatic scenes and beautiful colors. As the video evolves and changes along with the audio, the lighting effects vary and evolve along side of it.

Attendees are encouraged to bring pillows or blankets to get comfortable and relax while taking in the Audio Blanket Experience. The setting is ideal for meditation, brainstorming, relaxing or entertainment.

Audio Blanket has been performed at schools,artisan shops, ashrams, hookah lounges and more.

The Audio Blanket Experience was created and is performed by Cedrique Staples a musician and tech enthusiast.
